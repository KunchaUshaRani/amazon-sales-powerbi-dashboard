## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** built to analyze Amazon sales performance. The dashboard helps understand revenue trends, order status distribution, fulfillment performance, and regional sales patterns to support data-driven decision making.

---

## ⚠️ Problem Statement
Amazon sales data contains large volumes of transactional information such as order status, product categories, fulfillment methods, and shipping regions. However, analyzing raw data directly makes it difficult to identify revenue trends, cancellation risks, fulfillment efficiency, and regional sales disparities.

This project solves the problem by building an interactive dashboard that converts raw sales data into clear and actionable insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
• Analyze total revenue and total orders  
• Monitor order status distribution (Delivered, Cancelled, Pending, Returned, Shipped)  
• Identify top-performing product categories  
• Evaluate fulfillment performance (Amazon vs Merchant)  
• Analyze monthly revenue trends  
• Identify top-performing states by revenue  

---

## ❓ Business Questions
• What are the total revenue and total orders?  
• Which product categories generate the most revenue?  
• How do monthly sales trends change over time?  
• What is the cancellation rate of orders?  
• Which states generate the highest revenue?  
• How does fulfillment type impact revenue and cancellations?
